Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Notezilla

To upload resources, you need to create an account and log in. Then navigate to the "Upload" page, fill out the required information about your resource, select the file, and submit it for review. All uploads are moderated to ensure quality.

We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, JPG, and PNG files up to 10MB in size. These formats cover most study materials including notes, presentations, past papers, and diagrams.

Our moderation team typically reviews and approves content within 24-48 hours. During peak periods (like exam seasons), it may take up to 72 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your content is approved.

You can browse and view resource details without an account, but downloading requires registration. This helps us maintain quality, track usage, and provide better recommendations to our community.

Each resource page has a "Report" button. Click it and select the reason for reporting. Our moderation team will review the content within 24 hours and take appropriate action if necessary.
